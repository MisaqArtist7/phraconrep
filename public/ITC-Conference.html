ITC Conference Grants for COST Action Phraconrep (CA22115)

Are you a researcher or innovator, affiliated with an institution located in one of the Inclusive Target Countries (ITCs) or Near Neighbour Countries (NNCs)? If so, you could be eligible for an ITC Conference Grant offered by COST Action Phraconrep (CA22115).

Who Can Apply? ITC Conference Grants are designed to support researchers and innovators affiliated with

institutions in Inclusiveness Target Countries (ITCs) or Near Neighbour

Countries (NNCs). These grants provide financial assistance for presenting

their work at high-level international conferences not organized by the

COST Action. Notably, recent updates have expanded eligibility: Eligibility Expansion : Previously limited to young researchers under

40, ITC Conference Grants are now open to researchers and innovators of any age.

Presentation Requirement : The grants now support only those accepted for oral presentations, excluding poster presentations.The list of eligible countries includes:

ITC List : Albania, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Czech Republic, Estonia, Greece, North Macedonia, Hungary, Latvia, Lithuania, Malta, Moldova, Montenegro, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Serbia, Slovakia, Slovenia, Türkiye

NNC List : Algeria, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Egypt, Georgia, Jordan, Kosovo, Lebanon, Libya, Morocco, the Palestinian Authority, Syria, Tunisia, and Ukraine.

How to Apply

Applications must be submitted through the eCOST System. If you don’t have an eCOST Account yet, create one on eCOST System.

Required documentation includes:
  • ITC Conference Grant application form (available for download through the system)
  • Acceptance (or invitation) letter from conference organizers
  • Copy of abstract submitted to the conference
  • The applicant’s CV, including recent publications (limited to 1 page)
  • A list of expected expenses

Note: In the case of a joint presentation at a conference, only one presenter can be awarded a grant. By the time you submit your claim for reimbursement, you are required to submit a report, the certificate of attendance, programme of the conference or book of abstracts / proceedings indicating the oral presentation and a copy of your oral presentation.

The Core Group will evaluate the extent to which requested funds align with planned activities.

Call for Grants

Access to the call: CA22115- Call for Applications

We are pleased to announce the Call for Grants for Grant Period 2 for three networking activities organised through a Grant Awarding Process: YRI Conferences, ITC Conferences and Dissemination Conferences. Please note: The conference should end before October 2025. If you would like to attend a conference in October, please contact the Grant Awarding Coordinator first.

The attached document provide information on the eligibility criteria, duration and funding rules, information requested in the application procedure, the evaluation criteria concerning the proposal, and steps to follow in the Grant Awarding Process.

We would like to draw your attention to the fact that during this grant period, your applications can be submitted at any time until the 31st of July 2025 (subject to budget availability). However, the applications will be evaluated after three collection dates:

  • 20th of February 2025;
  • 31st of May 2025;
  • 31st of July 2025.

We warmly encourage you to participate and strengthen your work as well as the results of the network! We are excited to receive your applications.

To best prepare for applying, please read the Memorandum of Understanding and familiarise yourself with the COST Annotated Rules and the Grant Awarding User Guide.

If you need more information, please do not hesitate to contact Hana, our Grant Awarding Coordinator (Dissemination Conference Grants): hana.bergerova@ujep.cz or Ekaterina, our Vice-Coordinator responsible for YRI Conference Grants: ekaterina_gjergo@yahoo.co.uk..
