PRESS RELEASE

INTERVIEW WITH PHRACONREP MEMBER: HANNA SYTAR

Hanna Sytar, an active member of our COST Action, was recently featured in an interview on Radio Svoboda (in UKranian, with a Czech translation available), where she shared her expreiences leaving her home in UKraine and rebuilding her life in the Czech Republic. In discussing the challenges of displacement, she also highlights Phraconrep's role in facilitating collaboration and community for researchers in exile.
