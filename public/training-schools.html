Training Schools

COST Actions also facilitate knowledge sharing and collaborative efforts through the organization of training schools. These schools provide focused training, lasting up to 15 days, on specific topics related to the Action. Hosted on the site of one of the Action’s participants, these training sessions are primarily aimed at young researchers from all over Europe, though they are not limited to this group exclusively. Additionally, these training schools can offer retraining opportunities, aligning with the concept of lifelong learning.

As a COST Innovators Grant (CIG) tool, Training School (TS) is for Early Stage Researchers, especially PhD students and post-doctoral students, to provide them with high-quality training in line with the aims and targets of the COST Action that the applicant is a member of. For details, please read COST Annotated Rules: https://www.cost.eu/funding/how-to-get-funding/documents-guidelines
